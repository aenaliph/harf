The title of this blog حرف حیراں (harf-e hairaan) alludes to the entropy inherent in human languages. I intend this blog as a document for topics at the conjunction of linguistics, translation and language computing.


I am a doctoral candidate in Computational Linguistics at IIIT-Hyderabad. My research currently focuses on figurative language use in the context of Literary Machine Translation, but I also work around topics in machine translation post-editing and evaluation.


Prior to my present academic pursuits, I worked in the software industry in engineering and managerial roles for almost a decade, mostly in the areas of Machine Translation, Localization Engineering and Data Science.
