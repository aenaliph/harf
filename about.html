The title of this blog حرف حیراں (harf-e-hairaan) alludes to the entropy inherent in human languages. I intend this blog as a document for topics at the conjunction of language, linguistics and computing.


I am currently a doctoral candidate in Computational Linguistics at IIIT-Hyderabad. My research focuses on figurative language use in the context of Literary Machine Translation. I also work around topics in machine translation post-editing and evaluation.


Prior to my present academic pursuits, I worked in the software industry in engineering and managerial roles for almost a decade, mostly in the areas of Machine Translation, Localization Engineering and Data Science.
