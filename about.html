I am a doctoral candidate in Computational Linguistics at IIIT-Hyderabad. My research currently focuses on Literary Machine Translation but I am broadly interested in applying NLP techniques to figurative language use. I also work around topics in machine translation Postediting and Evaluation.


Prior to my present academic pursuits, I worked in the software industry in engineering and managerial roles for almost a decade, mostly in the areas of Machine Translation, Analytics, Globalization Engineering and Localization.


This blog (مہر نیم روز , mehr-e-neem-roz, which translates to noon-day-sun) is meant to document interesting cases of language use and technology implications that I come across. I also intend it as a learning aid – to document, classify and memorize topics, techniques and code.
